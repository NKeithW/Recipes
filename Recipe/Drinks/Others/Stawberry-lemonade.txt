buy some fresh strawberries and lemons. Chop up the strawberry into quarters and weigh them. Then weigh out equal parts sugar and stick them in an air tight container and stuck in the fridge for about a week. Strain out the syrup and mix 1oz, 3oz water (or sparkling water) and 4oz lemons juice and add sugar to your liking, or if you like it more stawberry swap the oz of the lemonade with strawberry oz.
