1 cup of sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup of margerine
1 cup of peanut butter, creamy
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
4 teaspoons of vanilla
3 1/2 cup sifted flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoons salt

1.  cream sugars, margerine, and peanut butter
2.  beat in eggs, milk, and vanilla
3.  sift together flour, baking soda, and salt
4.  stir the sifter mix into the egg mixture
5.  shape into balls and roll in sugar
6.  place on ungreased cookie sheet
7.  cook 10-12 minutes on 375
